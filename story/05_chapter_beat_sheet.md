# 05 Chapter Beat Sheet

## Structure
- Prologue + 32 chapters + epilogue
- Act I: Chapters 1-8
- Act II-A: Chapters 9-16
- Act II-B: Chapters 17-24
- Act III: Chapters 25-32

## Prologue
- A child watches black rain strike an empty granary while barges full of grain wait behind sealed floodgates.
- Purpose: establish motif and moral wound architecture.

## Act I - Fracture and Commitment

1. **Chapter 1 - The Ink That Doesn't Dry**
- Objective: introduce Serin and the oath bureaucracy.
- Conflict: relief permits denied by technicality.
- Reversal: a sealed emergency charter is placed in Serin's custody.
- Outcome: mission accepted.

2. **Chapter 2 - Witness Line**
- Objective: begin transport to Gloam Cliffs.
- Conflict: ambush by debt collectors.
- Reversal: Maela intervenes and insists on escorting Serin.
- Outcome: uneasy alliance.

3. **Chapter 3 - Lawful Harm**
- Objective: secure passage papers.
- Conflict: Serin must sign a denial harming a workers' camp.
- Reversal: Maela condemns her compliance.
- Outcome: character fault exposed.

4. **Chapter 4 - The River That Lied**
- Objective: cross Mirror March safely.
- Conflict: water-level records contradict weather.
- Reversal: evidence suggests data manipulation.
- Outcome: first systemic suspicion.

5. **Chapter 5 - Blank Witness**
- Objective: inspect charter authenticity.
- Conflict: missing witness field appears impossible.
- Reversal: Serin dismisses concern to preserve mission integrity.
- Outcome: delayed truth.

6. **Chapter 6 - Debt Gate**
- Objective: enter Synod district.
- Conflict: toll enforcers demand blood collateral.
- Reversal: Maela trades her oath-ring mark for entry.
- Outcome: relational debt forms.

7. **Chapter 7 - The Regents' Hand**
- Objective: deliver charter to Provost Cael.
- Conflict: Serin notices signature anomalies across regent archives.
- Reversal: Cael praises her and redirects her to emergency council.
- Outcome: proximity to power.

8. **Chapter 8 - Charter of Ash**
- Objective: witness ratification.
- Conflict: charter text publicly read.
- Reversal (Twist 1): charter enables martial ration doctrine, not relief.
- Outcome: Serin chooses to steal a duplicate and flee with Maela.

## Act II-A - Ascent and Poisoned Midpoint

9. **Chapter 9 - Fugitive Geometry**
- Objective: evade Synod pursuit.
- Conflict: oath marks track their route.
- Reversal: Toma offers canal cover in exchange for future exposure of Synod records.
- Outcome: temporary sanctuary.

10. **Chapter 10 - Burn Ledger**
- Objective: decode supply ledger.
- Conflict: ledger encrypted in legal shorthand.
- Reversal: Maela reveals hidden mapping skills from her captain years.
- Outcome: deeper trust.

11. **Chapter 11 - Night Weirs**
- Objective: inspect floodgates at midnight.
- Conflict: patrols and silent gate movement.
- Reversal: gates open against weather logic.
- Outcome: proof of artificial scarcity grows.

12. **Chapter 12 - Memory Toll**
- Objective: buy archive access from Mirror Court.
- Conflict: broker demands memory collateral.
- Reversal: Serin pays with memory of her brother's last words.
- Outcome: emotional cost and partial key obtained.

13. **Chapter 13 - The Healer's Quarter**
- Objective: gather civilian testimony.
- Conflict: starving district refuses officials.
- Reversal: Maela confesses her past floodgate crime.
- Outcome: district grants evidence.

14. **Chapter 14 - Open Cistern**
- Objective: move evidence to Commons council.
- Conflict: sabotage in canal tunnels.
- Reversal: evidence survives but implicates Commons intermediaries too.
- Outcome: moral complexity expands.

15. **Chapter 15 - Glass Court Hearing**
- Objective: force public arbitration.
- Conflict: all factions attempt narrative capture.
- Reversal: High Reader Ys leaks hidden amendment references.
- Outcome: path to Charter Engine revealed.

16. **Chapter 16 - The Machine Under Drought**
- Objective: infiltrate engine access chamber.
- Conflict: legal lock sequence requires official signatures.
- Reversal (Twist 2): engine intentionally cycles scarcity by law.
- Outcome: "victory with poison" - truth known, power still held by Synod.

## Act II-B - Collapse and Reconstruction

17. **Chapter 17 - Riot of Receipts**
- Objective: publish proof.
- Conflict: public unrest turns indiscriminate.
- Reversal: Commons leaders call for total gate seizure.
- Outcome: risk of civil war.

18. **Chapter 18 - Terms of Mercy**
- Objective: craft non-war intervention plan.
- Conflict: no faction trusts neutral process.
- Reversal: Serin proposes transparent gate lottery as stopgap.
- Outcome: partial buy-in.

19. **Chapter 19 - Captain Without Crest**
- Objective: secure former flood crews.
- Conflict: crews blame Maela for old catastrophe.
- Reversal: she accepts public guilt without defense.
- Outcome: one crew rejoins reluctantly.

20. **Chapter 20 - Decoy Oath**
- Objective: prepare engine strike route.
- Conflict: reader sees Maela make secret deal with Toma.
- Reversal (dramatic irony): apparent betrayal setup.
- Outcome: trust tension escalates.

21. **Chapter 21 - Court of Smoke**
- Objective: negotiate temporary truce.
- Conflict: Mirror Court demands permanent trade monopoly.
- Reversal: Serin refuses and loses key support.
- Outcome: coalition cracks.

22. **Chapter 22 - Portraits That Repeat**
- Objective: decode regent lineage records.
- Conflict: archives purged in real time.
- Reversal: surviving portraits reveal identical rhetorical patterns over centuries.
- Outcome: foreshadow Twist 4.

23. **Chapter 23 - False Route**
- Objective: enter weir tunnels.
- Conflict: strike team ambushes Serin using leaked route.
- Reversal: Maela is seen riding with Synod escorts.
- Outcome: emotional collapse.

24. **Chapter 24 - Ash Oath**
- Objective: survive capture.
- Conflict: Serin believes betrayal complete.
- Reversal (Twist 3): Maela's "betrayal" diverted escorts away from civilian wards; she frees Serin at great personal cost.
- Outcome: trust rebuilt under fire.

## Act III - Integration and Costly Victory

25. **Chapter 25 - Floodline Pact**
- Objective: rebuild coalition for final push.
- Conflict: factions demand dominance clauses.
- Reversal: Serin threatens to publish every faction crime at once.
- Outcome: fragile operational alliance.

26. **Chapter 26 - The Third Key**
- Objective: obtain final lock key.
- Conflict: key encoded in Serin's sacrificed memory gap.
- Reversal: Elder Naev reconstructs memory through witness ritual.
- Outcome: path to engine opens.

27. **Chapter 27 - Weirfall**
- Objective: storm the Hollow Weirs.
- Conflict: provost militia and collapsing channels.
- Reversal: Maela is severely wounded holding the gate line.
- Outcome: Serin reaches core alone.

28. **Chapter 28 - Charter Fire**
- Objective: disable engine.
- Conflict: shutdown would trigger immediate flood surge.
- Reversal: engine can be rewritten, not merely destroyed.
- Outcome: harder moral choice emerges.

29. **Chapter 29 - The Name Ledger**
- Objective: write new charter conditions.
- Conflict: legal medium demands a permanent witness sacrifice.
- Reversal: Serin volunteers her claim to office and inheritance rights.
- Outcome: personal power surrendered.

30. **Chapter 30 - Refusal**
- Objective: finalize signature chain.
- Conflict: provost offers Serin controlled regency.
- Reversal: she refuses lawful domination and signs decentralized water rights instead.
- Outcome: parallel inversion of Ch. 3.

31. **Chapter 31 - The Regent Seat**
- Objective: end provost control.
- Conflict: Cael tries to retain authority.
- Reversal (Twist 4): the regent seat itself compels authoritarian continuity; removing Cael alone solves nothing.
- Outcome: seat is severed from engine and authority redistributed.

32. **Chapter 32 - Rain Without Permission**
- Objective: survive aftermath and stabilize city.
- Conflict: power vacuum and revenge threats.
- Reversal: neighborhoods self-organize gate councils faster than elites expected.
- Outcome: basin stabilizes with imperfect but real civic control.

## Epilogue
- Serin and Maela stand at a reopened flood stair as outer envoys arrive with treaties bearing unfamiliar oath scripts.
- Hook: the basin's liberation has awakened older engines beyond the mountains.
